Help

If you need assistance with using the Weather Tracker app, follow these steps:

  • To add a city, type the name in the form and click "Add City".
  • To remove a city, click the "Delete" button next to the city's information.
  • If the city name is not found, an error message will be displayed.

For further help, contact support at support@weathertracker.com.
